Research Experience (PhD)

Research conducted at LMAP (UPPA) on the local reconstruction of conservative fluxes from finite element solutions, in the presence of complex geometric interfaces and discontinuous diffusion coefficients. Work focused on using these reconstructed fluxes to develop robust and efficient a posteriori error estimators, with applications to adaptive meshing strategies.
